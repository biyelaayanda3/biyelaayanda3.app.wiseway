Pychometric Assessment

This programme uses both computerised and written psychometric assessment tools to evaluate your aptitude, interests, personality and values.

The Psychometric Career Assessments offered by the Counselling & Careers Development Unit are suitable for the following individuals:

  • Grade 9 learners having to make subject choice decisions
  • Grade 11 & 12 considering post-school options
  • Current university students
  • Adults considering mid-career changes
